NASA's James Webb Space Telescope (JWST) has been hit by a micrometeoroid, which has damaged one of the 18 beryllium-gold segments that makes up its main 6.5-meter mirror. It happened between May 23 and 25 according to NASA with the result being a “marginally detectable effect in the data.”
